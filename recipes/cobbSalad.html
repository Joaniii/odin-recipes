Cobb Salad

Main Page

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 3 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • Â¾ cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Ranch-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a sauce pan with cold water, boil them, and let them cool for 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. Place beacon in a large skillet; cook it for 7-10 min; drain , cumble, and set aside.
  3. Divide shredded lettuce among individual plates. Top with rows of bacon, eggs, chicken, tomatoes, blue cheese, green onions, and avocado. Drizzle with dressing.
  4. Enjoy!!!
